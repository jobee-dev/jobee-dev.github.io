Dispatch Commander

Take command of your city's emergency services. Dispatch units, manage resources, and tackle dynamic incidents in this real-time strategy simulation.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Incident Management: Respond to dynamically generated incidents, from minor calls to major multi-agency emergencies, each with unique requirements and priorities.
  • Strategic Unit Deployment: Manage a diverse fleet of vehicles (patrol cars, fire trucks, ambulances, specialized units). Calculate optimal routes and dispatch units effectively across the city using an interactive map interface.
  • Station Management & Expansion: Build and upgrade police stations, firehouses, and ambulance depots. Increase capacity, expand coverage radius, and improve maintenance efficiency.
  • Resource & Budget Control: Earn funds by successfully resolving incidents. Manage your budget to purchase new units, upgrade stations, and maintain your fleet's operational readiness.
  • Interactive Map Interface: Visualize units, stations, and active incidents on a detailed city map. Track unit movements and coverage areas in real-time.
  • Diverse Vehicle Fleet: Command a wide range of standard and specialized vehicles, including K9 units, HazMat trucks, Mobile ICUs, SWAT vehicles, and more, each suited for specific incident types.
  • Unit Customization: Personalize your units with custom callsigns for a more immersive command experience.
